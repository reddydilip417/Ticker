What is E-Summit ?

E-Summit will be a two day event at RV College of Engineering to spread awareness about entrepreneurship and help the aspiring entrepreneurs to take the next step towards their dream. We are also aiming at bringing all the startups from RVCE (including alumini) under a single umbrella. E-Summit will be a platform for all likeminded people to interact, learn about enntreprenurship. The best from the industry including the CEO's,founders of big startups will be present to address the gathering.

Who we are

Entrepreneurship Cell is setup with the primary motive of encouraging entrepreneurship amongst the students of RVCE. It was observed that most of our fellow students opt for placements or chalk out their higher studies, but few take entrepreneurship seriously as a career option. To be an entrepreneur doesn't mean starting a new company but the basic approach is to have an innovative idea, starting from scratch and then building on it.

Risk taking is inherent in starting anything new, untried and untested. It is this element of risk that teaches us a valuable lesson in life - "Everything comes at a price". This is exactly what we plan to do. The cell imparts skills and knowledge that will relevance in the real world. Not everyone will be lucky enough to become a successful entrepreneur. But what is learnt will be with you forever - and take you where no book prescribed in your syllabus can.
